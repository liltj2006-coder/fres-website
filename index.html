About FRES

Serious About
Commercial Real Estate

The Fisher Real Estate Society is Ohio State's premier student organization dedicated to commercial real estate. We exist to bridge the gap between the classroom and the boardroom — connecting ambitious students with the industry's most influential professionals.

Commercial real estate is one of the world's largest asset classes, touching every sector of the economy. FRES prepares its members to enter this industry with real skills, real relationships, and a real competitive edge.

Membership is selective. Our application and interview process ensures every member is committed, capable, and ready to represent Ohio State in the CRE industry.

Our Mission

To develop the next generation of commercial real estate leaders through education, professional development, and industry immersion — rooted in the academic excellence of Fisher College of Business.

Why Commercial Real Estate?

CRE encompasses office, industrial, retail, multifamily, and hospitality assets. It sits at the intersection of finance, development, and urban strategy — offering career paths in acquisitions, development, brokerage, and capital markets.

The FRES Difference

We go beyond theory. Members participate in site tours, industry panels, and recruiting pipelines that lead directly to internships and full-time roles at top CRE firms nationally.

Join FRES

The Application
Process

Membership in FRES is earned, not given. We recruit students who are serious about commercial real estate — academically strong, professionally motivated, and ready to contribute to a high-caliber organization.

1

Submit Application

Complete our written application covering your background, interests, and goals in CRE. Applications open each semester.

2

First-Round Interview

A 20-minute conversation with two members of the executive board. We want to understand who you are and why CRE.

3

Final Interview

Selected candidates advance to a final round with the full board. Technical and behavioral questions included.

4

Offer & Onboarding

Accepted members receive an offer and are onboarded into the organization ahead of the following semester.

Who We're Looking For

There's no single profile. We value diverse backgrounds, disciplines, and perspectives. What unites every FRES member is drive.

  • Genuine interest in commercial real estate
  • Strong academic standing at Ohio State
  • Professional ambition and self-motivation
  • Willingness to contribute to the organization
  • Ability to work in a team environment
  • Open to any major — Finance, Real Estate, Engineering, Architecture, and more
